How to order forklift parts.

Send the details, review the quote, approve the order, then choose pickup or delivery. The steps below show what happens on a normal enquiry.

How we work

Four simple steps.

STEP 01Start

Send details

Call, email or use the form with the model, serial number, OEM number or photos.

STEP 02Check

Confirm fitment

We check the information and ask for anything missing before quoting.

STEP 03Quote

Review quote

You receive price, availability, lead time and delivery options.

STEP 04Order

Dispatch

After approval and payment, confirmed parts are arranged for pickup or delivery.

Commercial terms

Clear terms.

Quotation

Quotes are sent by email with part reference, price, lead time and fitment notes. Purchase order details can be included if needed.

Payment

Payment method and release timing are confirmed on the quote. One-off orders are normally prepared after payment confirmation.

Delivery

Delivery depends on availability and destination. Sydney delivery, courier and pickup options are shown on the quote.

Returns

If a part supplied against a verified enquiry doesn't fit, return it within 14 days in original condition for replacement or refund.
