About Us

Built for Forklift Operators

YL Specialist Engineering Pty Ltd is a Sydney-based supplier focused on Toyota forklift service parts, especially the 8FG, 8FB and 7FG series used across Australian warehouses, workshops and logistics sites.

Founded by Dr. Louis Liu, the business combines engineering judgement with practical sourcing. We help customers identify compatible OEM-quality alternatives and avoid the cost and delay of ordering the wrong part.

Whether you manage a fleet or need one filter urgently, the service is built around clear communication, careful part matching and fast fulfilment.
