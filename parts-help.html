Parts Assistance

Useful When the Part Number Is Missing.

A lot of forklift parts enquiries start with a worn part, a blurry plate or a machine model. This page explains exactly what to send.

What to Send

The more details you provide, the faster we can narrow down the correct replacement.

ID
Machine identityForklift brand, model and serial number.
NO
Part numbersOEM number or supplier number if visible.
PH
PhotosClear photos of the old part from multiple angles.
DEL
Delivery needQuantity required and delivery suburb or postcode.

What We Check

We look for practical replacement options without losing sight of fitment risk.

XR
Cross-referenceOEM numbers and suitable replacement options.
FIT
Fitment detailsVoltage, thread, seal, filter and dimensional details.
ETA
AvailabilityStock location, lead time and courier options.
ALT
AlternativesOther parts when the first option is unavailable.
Best photos: front, side, any label or stamp, and the old part next to a ruler or coin for scale.
