About YL

A Specialist Parts Desk for Forklift Operators.

YL combines engineering judgement with practical parts sourcing for Australian workshops, warehouses and fleet operators.

Company

YL Specialist Engineering Pty Ltd

YL is a Sydney-based Toyota forklift parts supplier with a strong focus on service parts, especially the 8FG, 8FB and 7FG series used across Australian warehouses, workshops and logistics sites.

Founded by Dr. Louis Liu, the business is built around a simple goal: help customers identify the right parts, avoid ordering mistakes and keep forklifts moving.
